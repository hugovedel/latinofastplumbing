Privacy Policy

Latino Fast Plumbing · Chicago, IL
Last updated: March 29, 2026

Latino Fast Plumbing ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you contact us through WhatsApp, our website, or any other communication channel.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number (WhatsApp or other)
  • Home or business address in Chicago, IL
  • Description of your plumbing issue or service request
  • Preferred appointment date and time
  • Communication history through WhatsApp

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Schedule and confirm plumbing service appointments
  • Contact you to confirm, modify, or follow up on your service request
  • Send you service reminders or updates
  • Improve the quality of our customer service

We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

3. WhatsApp Communication

Our virtual assistant Hugo operates through the WhatsApp Business API provided by Meta. When you send us a message via WhatsApp, your messages are processed by our automated system to assist you with scheduling and inquiries. Your conversation data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized Latino Fast Plumbing staff.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With our plumbing technicians to fulfill your service appointment
  • When required by law or legal process
  • With service providers who help us operate our business (e.g., cloud storage), under strict confidentiality agreements

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. WhatsApp conversation history is retained for up to 24 hours in our automated systems for continuity of service. Appointment records are kept for up to 2 years for business and legal purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our systems use industry-standard encryption and security protocols.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

10. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Illinois, United States. Any disputes arising from this policy shall be resolved in the courts of Cook County, Illinois.
